Wireframes in UI Design

Wireframes are used to visualize the layout of a UI Design. There are three types of wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframe
Low-fidelity wireframes are simple sketches that are used to visualize the layout of a UI Design
Mid-fidelity wireframe
Mid-fidelity wireframes are more detailed than low-fidelity wireframes and are used to show the layout and content of a UI Design
High-fidelity wireframe
High-fidelity wireframes are detailed and realistic representations of a UI Design and are usd to show the layout and final ui Design
