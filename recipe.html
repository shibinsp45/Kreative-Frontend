Indian Recipe for Chicken Curry

- Steps to cook a chicken curry
  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add chopped onion, garlic, ginger and green chilli
  3. Saute till onion turns golden brown
  4. Add chopped tomato and cook till oil separates
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook till chicken is tender
  6. Add spices and cook for 5 minutes
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves
Enjoy your chicken curry with fried rice
